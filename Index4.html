Iceland

From my holiday in Spring 2019 with my girlfriend, we visited in Iceland. One of the most beautiful places in the world, no doubt! We were there only four days,
but still we were able to see a lot of things in there. Nature is something very different comparing to Finland. Even though it rained everyday, it did not stop us!.
I totally recommend to visit there, if you have a chance. Also, rent a car. It will help you visit places you can't visit any other way.
